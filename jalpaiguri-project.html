Infradhip is proud to embark on an impactful gas pipeline construction project in the enchanting region of Jalpaiguri, West Bengal. This endeavor is a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence and our esteemed partnership with Tridevvr Construction Private Limited, as designated by the prestigious client, Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited.

Project Overview:
Location: Jalpaiguri, West Bengal, India
Client: Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited
Scope: Construction of a 4-inch diameter gas pipeline, a vital link in the region's energy infrastructure.

Commitment to Excellence:
As the entrusted construction partner for this project, we shoulder the responsibility for every aspect of the pipeline's construction, from initial conception to the final commissioning. Our dedication to engineering precision, safety, and environmental stewardship is the driving force behind every decision we make.
